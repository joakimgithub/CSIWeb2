Help us become even better!

Our Clients are the most important source for our continued improvement and development. Therefore we ask you to tell us what you consider to be the most important criteria for the success of delivering the engagement. When it is time to follow-up the engagement we will ask you to evaluate to what degree we have met your expectations. This simple routine is an important part of our Quality System. Examples of quality criteria can be:



  • Keep agreed delivery times
  • Correct match of skillls and experience
  • Ability to work well with the client
  • Effective and speedy project follow-up
  • Support/service from Sogeti organisation
  • Keep agreed costs
  • Effective consultants
  • Good documentation
  • Well-structured programs
  • Effective work-methods
  • Useful results
  • High accessibility
  • Keep promises
  • Positive attitude
  • Maintain agreed service levels
  • Pro activity
  • Sensitive to Client's requirements
  • Deliver expected functionality
  • Effective problem solving

OTACE

On Time Above Client Expectation (OTACE) is the program used by Sogeti to help benchmark the satisfaction of its Clients in order to improve Sogeti's quality standards and meet Client's expectations




This OTACE document and other statements made as part of the OTACE program do not form part of Client contracts and are remitted by Clients to Sogeti for internal information purposes only. They shall not create any benefits or burdens for Sogeti or its Clients.
